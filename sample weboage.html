About Us

ORCO organic spices are farmed using natural fertilizers like manure and compost and not chemical pesticides and harmful chemicals. These make the organic herbs and spices much more nutritious which are better for health. Chemical pesticides and fertilisers have harmful substances in them that can cause many types of diseases in the long run. This includes harmful diseases like cancer, birth defects, disorders, digestive problems, and even premature death. Fresh Herbs and Spices Spices and herbs have to be fresh as they lose their essential oils and flavour if they sit in a bottle for months. Organic herbs and spices have to be consumed in a limited period because there is low number of chemical fertilisers and preservatives in these products. This means that organic herbs and spices are fresher than their conventional counterparts and are better for health.
